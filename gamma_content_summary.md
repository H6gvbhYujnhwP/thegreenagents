## Presentation 2: Email Marketing
**URL:** https://why-email-marketing-stil-xbtitsy.gamma.site/

### Key Messages:
- **ROI:** Email marketing generates £30-£40 for every £1 spent
- **Highest ROI channel** for service businesses
- Email builds trust and relationships that convert prospects into clients

### Core Arguments:

**1. Social Media Creates Awareness. Email Creates Connection.**
- Social: Fast-paced, algorithm-driven, public broadcasting
- Email: Personal, direct, permission-based communication
- "Social introduces you. Email helps people choose you."

**2. You Own Your Email List (Not Social Platforms)**
- Email list is a business asset you control
- Social following is owned by the platform
- No algorithm limiting visibility
- Portable across platforms
- Increases business valuation

**3. Email Offers Depth, Not Distraction**
- Social: 2-3 seconds of attention
- Email: Several minutes of focused reading
- Space for insights, stories, case studies
- Proper explanation of complex services

**4. Prospects Buy When THEY Are Ready**
- B2B decisions take weeks, months, or years
- Email keeps you top-of-mind throughout journey
- Triggering moments: crisis, deadlines, budget availability, growth phase
- Consistency matters more than frequency

**5. Use Email to Educate Your Market**
- Explain full service range
- Demystify your process
- Educate about pricing
- Address common objections
- Build authority and trust

### Services to Highlight:
- Email automation
- Lead nurturing campaigns
- Customer follow-ups
- Personalized outreach
- Email sequence creation
- Performance optimization
