# The Green Agents - SEO & Marketing Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive SEO and marketing strategy for The Green Agents website, integrating content from the Gamma presentations and optimizing for AI automation services targeting UK SMEs.

## Target Audience

**Primary:** UK SME owners and decision-makers who are:
- Wearing multiple hats (CEO, Sales, Marketing)
- Time-poor and resource-constrained
- Looking for marketing automation solutions
- Frustrated with inconsistent marketing results
- Seeking predictable lead generation

**Secondary:**
- Marketing managers in small businesses
- Service business owners (consultants, coaches, agencies)
- Companies looking to scale without hiring
